About Beth

Movement is nutrition for the body, mind & spirit. I prescribe movements that benefit your individual needs the most. I am committed to helping normal people and their bodies navigate life effectively and free of pain through implementation of scientifically sound programming and assessments. My professional interests include mobility & joint health, endurance sports, general health & well-being, as well as promoting health and wellness at any size, age or ability. By combining extensive knowledge of human physiology & anatomy with common sense, I guarantee a safe space for growing and trying new movements and habits.
